Full Resume

Research

Guray Ozen is a compiler engineer in the Machine Learning Compiler team at NVIDIA. His work focuses on optimizing compilers and programming languages for GPU utilization in machine learning and high‑performance computing.

He has contributed to production‑grade compilers such as Clang, Flang, MLIR, IREE, and NVIDIA HPC (compilers).

Previously he served on the OpenMP Language Committee for NVIDIA and contributed extensively to the OpenACC specification.

Current Projects at NVIDIA

  • Cutlass Python DSL – Python DSL for composing high‑performance GPU kernels with CUTLASS.
  • TileIR (coming soon) – MLIR‑based IR for portable GPU programming and kernel composition across ML & HPC.
