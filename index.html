Research

Guray Ozen is a compiler software engineer at NVIDIA. His current research focus is on how the C, C++, Fortran languages, and OpenMP/OpenACC programming models should evolve to take advantage of the GPUs in the HPC area. He has made significant contributions to many production compilers such as clang, flang, and NVIDIA HPC compilers (formerly PGI compilers).
